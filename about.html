About Me

Hi, I'm a technology enthusiast currently exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence, virtualization, and workflow optimization.

Driven by a practical approach to productivity, I built TaskSpace as an exercise in designing streamlined management tools. I enjoy experimenting with different organizational methodologies—balancing structured Kanban boards for milestone tracking with lightweight, customizable checklists for day-to-day operations.

Beyond front-end design, my interests extend into home-lab administration and infrastructure. I enjoy tinkering with self-hosted setups, managing resource allocations, and configuring hypervisors like Proxmox to deploy tailored utilities. Whether it's refactoring web interfaces or optimizing system hardware, I am always focused on finding efficient, practical solutions to everyday technical challenges.
